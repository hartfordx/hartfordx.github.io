About Me

My Story

Throughout my time at UMass Amherst, I've become interested in how economic analysis and technology can be applied to real problems, especially in public and municipal settings. I enjoy working with others and have liked collaborating with town staff, community members, and classmates on projects that have practical, real-world outcomes. These experiences have helped shape my interest in planning-related and public-sector careers.

Outside of school, I enjoy golfing, skateboarding, fitness, and spending time with friends and family. I also like to travel and explore new places.
